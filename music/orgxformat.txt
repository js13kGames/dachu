orgx, extremely tiny org clone

header
------
TTT - tempo in ascii numbers
MM - music ends, loop to specificed measure
MM - mesaure to loop back to in ascii numbers

data
------
32[IT] - switch instrument
33[MD] - switch mode (0 - regular volume, 1 - decay)
34[LT] - rest length
[NN][LL] - note and length (0 = C1)

converting from org to orgx
---------------------------
there is currently no program to convert from org to orgx
you must manually convert each note in a hex editor like I did
